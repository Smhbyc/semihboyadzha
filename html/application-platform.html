Methodist University International Students Application Platform

Created On April 23, 2018

The design of a new communication website between the prospective students and the Methodist University recruitment team which combines the university needs as well as creating a platform for the prospective students to have fun while applying to Methodist University.

The web application is aligned to be consistent with look-and-feel of the students. The website is designed based on the needs of the international programs office recruitment team while considering the university’s identity standards. The description will explain the website functionalities such as, home, contacts, and about pages. Most importantly it will be based on the “Reflex Game” that prospective students will play and send the result as their application assessment to the international programs office recruitment team.

The project is completed using front-end design and development using Bootstrap 4.

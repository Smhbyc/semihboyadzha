Within You

Created On April 24, 2020

Every person in our lives have their own story. Their own battle to fight for. Sometimes, our stories comes with easy and the days get better in time. However, there are times in our lives when we feel vulnerable, easily knocked over, easily broken.

In those dark times, there are two main factors that keeps us hopeful, that give us reasons to fight for. Our inner-self and external support group whether it is our friends or family.

What’s done is done. What’s gone is gone. One of life’s lessons is always moving on. It’s okay to look back to see how far you’ve come but keep moving forward. – Roy T. Bennett

The dark times are not always easy to get over. Belive me, some of them take a while to let go or learn from them. However, if you only keep pushing yourself, if you keep fighting for your own growth and future, eventually you will get through all that life throwns in front of you.

In this work of mine (featuring Yana Marchenko) I wanted to portray the different emotional boundaries of how we feel during our highs and lows. You can reach to the video with the link down below.
